# Cybercrime Case Study: Phishing Attack

**Type of Cybercrime:** Phishing  

**Scenario:**  
A student at VIT Bhopal received an email claiming they had won a scholarship from a reputed organization. The email instructed the student to provide personal details, including college login credentials and bank account information, to claim the prize. Believing the email to be genuine, the student submitted the information. Within hours, unauthorized transactions appeared in their bank account.

**How it Happened (Step-by-Step):**  
1. The student receives an email with official-looking branding and a convincing message.  
2. The email asks for sensitive information, creating urgency (“Claim your prize within 24 hours”).  
3. The student fills out the form and submits personal and banking details.  
4. Fraudsters use the submitted information to withdraw money or access accounts.  

**Who is Targeted:** Students, young adults, or anyone unfamiliar with phishing scams.  

**Consequences:**  
- Financial loss  
- Compromised personal accounts  
- Emotional stress and loss of trust  
- Possible identity theft  

**Lesson Learned:** Always verify the sender and avoid sharing sensitive information online without confirmation.
