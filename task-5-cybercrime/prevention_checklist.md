# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. Never click on links from unknown or suspicious emails, WhatsApp messages, or social media.  
2. Verify the sender before sharing personal or financial information.  
3. Use strong, unique passwords for every account and change them regularly.  
4. Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on email, banking apps, and social media accounts.  
5. Avoid downloading attachments or files from untrusted sources.  
6. Only use **official UPI/payment apps** and double-check the payee before confirming transactions.  
7. Regularly monitor your bank statements and online accounts for unusual activity.  
8. Report cybercrimes immediately via:  
   - **National Cyber Crime Portal:** [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in/)  
   - **Helpline:** 1930 (available 24x7)
